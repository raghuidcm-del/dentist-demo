Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers to help you understand your dental journey.

Is a Root Canal Treatment painful?

Not at all! With modern local anesthesia and advanced rotary endodontics at our clinic, a root canal is as comfortable and painless as getting a routine filling.

How long do dental implants last?

With proper oral hygiene and regular dental check-ups, dental implants can last a lifetime. They act and function exactly like your natural teeth.

Do you offer EMI options for treatments?

Yes, we offer flexible 0% interest EMI options for extensive treatments like Braces, Aligners, and full-mouth Dental Implants to make premium care affordable.

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend a routine dental check-up and professional cleaning every 6 months to prevent cavities, gum disease, and maintain optimal oral health.
